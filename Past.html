Past Ariana

Ariana Grande hasn intriguing past once you learn about her work

Ariana Grande-Butera was born on June 26, 1993, in Boca Raton, Florida.
As a young child, Grande performed with the Fort Lauderdale Children's
Theater, playing her first role as the title character in their version
of the musical Annie. After various musicals and fame, she later became
a star under Nickoleden and worked on two shows named Victorious and
Sam and Cat. Grande was cast in the Nickelodeon television show Victorious
along with 13 co-star Elizabeth Gillies in 2009. The show premiered on March 
2010 to the second-largest audience for a live-action series in 
Nickelodeon, with 5.7 million viewers.After the first season of 
Victorious wrapped, Grande wanted to focus on her music career and began
working on her debut album in August 2010. After four seasons, Victorious
was not renewed, with the finale airing in February 2013. Nickelodeon created 
Sam & Cat, an iCarly and Victorious spin-off starring Jennette McCurdy 
and Grande started her solo career after being done with Nickelodeon. Grande
recorded her debut studio album Yours Truly, originally titled
Daydreamin', over three years. Grande released her second studio album, My 
Everything on August 25, 2014, and debuted atop the Billboard 200. Its 
lead single "Problem" featured Australian rapper Iggy Azalea and 
premiered at the 2014 Radio Disney Music Awards on April 26, 2014.
